Created.

toj v0.1:

Fastest conversation model trained with 94905 conversation data. Leveraging Jaccard Similarity and Jaro-winkler Similarity to choose the correct response. Jaccard Similarity is used to filter most of the conversation data, then Jaro-winkler Similarity will choose the best response from filtered conversation data.
